Things I've Built

Each of these started with a real problem I was hitting at work. The operations experience came first. The software came after.

01

FixHub

Maintenance ticketing built for property managers.

No more WhatsApp threads. No more missed jobs. FixHub gives property managers a structured way to log maintenance requests, assign them to technicians, track status, and close them with documentation. It was built because I spent years watching jobs disappear into group chats - and watching that directly translate to unhappy guests and unhappy owners.

Deployed on a live portfolio of 350+ units. The result was a 40% reduction in average maintenance response time - not because the contractors got faster, but because the coordination got cleaner.

02

GuestCare

Guest communication platform for holiday home operators.

Centralises messaging across Airbnb, Booking.com, and WhatsApp into one place. Holiday home operators currently jump between three or four apps to answer the same guest's questions - and response time is one of the top factors in review scores.

GuestCare also handles the guest journey: pre-arrival instructions, check-in guide, issue reporting during the stay, check-out reminders, and review follow-up. Everything a guest needs from booking to departure, in one flow.

03

PaMarket

"Konke Endaweni Eyodwa" - Everything In One Place.

Online marketplace for Zimbabwe. Buy, sell, find jobs - all in one place. The Zimbabwean market has been underserved by e-commerce for years. Local buyers and sellers have had to rely on fragmented Facebook groups and WhatsApp broadcasts, with no real structure, no search, no categories.

PaMarket is built specifically for that market: the browsing behaviour, the payment realities, the types of goods people actually trade. The tagline is in Ndebele - "Everything In One Place" - because that's what it's actually trying to do. It's live and open to users.

04

GGS Platform

Property management platform - live at globalgamazine.co.za

Built for property management companies running multiple units. Bookings, maintenance, and financials in one dashboard. Most PMS software on the market is either too expensive, too generic, or built for hotel groups - not for holiday home operators managing a mixed portfolio in Dubai.

GGS is designed from the operator's perspective - because I am one. The platform is live and deployed for Global Gamazine Solutions, a real client in South Africa. Covers solutions, project showcase, process documentation, and an epoxy flooring services module.
