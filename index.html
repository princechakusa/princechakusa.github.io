Guest Reviews Dashboard

52 reviews · 40 properties · 4.5 avg rating

Problem

Guest review data sat in raw Airbnb/Booking.com exports. Managers had no way to spot trends or flag low ratings across 40 properties without hours of manual Excel work each week.

Solution

Built a custom web dashboard with a client-side data import system, Chart.js visualisations, and date-range filtering. Designed specifically for a non-technical operations team with no backend required.

Outcome

Review monitoring time dropped from ~3 hours/week to under 15 minutes. Managers could identify property-specific rating drops within 24 hours of guest checkout.

FixHub — Maintenance Operations

400+ units · 16 active tickets · Supabase-powered

Problem

Maintenance across 400+ units managed via WhatsApp groups. Tickets got lost, technicians had no structured assignment, SLA compliance was unmeasurable, management had no visibility.

Solution

Designed a full-stack system with Supabase as the real-time database. Built structured ticket schema, role-based access for Facilities Manager vs Technician, and SLA calculation logic against target resolution times.

Outcome

Maintenance visibility went from zero to complete. Management could see live SLA breach rates, identify bottleneck technicians, and proactively manage stock alerts — driving measurable improvements in resolution times.
