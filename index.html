Guest Reviews Dashboard

52 reviews · 40 properties · 4.5 avg rating

Problem

Guest review data sat in raw exports. Managers had no way to spot trends or flag low ratings across 40 properties without hours of manual Excel work each week.

Solution

Custom web dashboard with client-side data import, Chart.js visualisations, and date-range filtering. Designed for a non-technical operations team with no backend required.

Outcome

Review monitoring time dropped from ~3 hours/week to under 15 minutes. Managers identified property-specific rating drops within 24 hours of guest checkout.

FixHub — Maintenance Ops

400+ units · 16 active tickets · Supabase-powered

Problem

Maintenance across 400+ units via WhatsApp groups. Tickets lost, no structured assignment, SLA compliance unmeasurable, zero management visibility.

Solution

Full-stack system with Supabase real-time DB. Structured ticket schema, role-based access for Manager vs Technician, and SLA calculation logic against target resolution times.

Outcome

Maintenance visibility went from zero to complete. Live SLA breach rates, bottleneck identification, proactive stock management — measurable improvement in resolution times.
